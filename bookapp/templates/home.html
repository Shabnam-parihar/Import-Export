About Us

Slaavno is an India-based import–export trading company connecting reliable Indian manufacturers, suppliers, farmers, and artisans with businesses in the United Kingdom. We act as a trusted sourcing partner, helping UK buyers access quality Indian products with confidence and ease.

We simplify the sourcing process by managing supplier identification, product sourcing, price negotiations, quality coordination, and export documentation. Our objective is to make importing from India transparent, efficient, and fully compliant with UK import standards.

Slaavno operates as a merchant exporter, offering sourcing support across multiple product categories. If a product is not currently listed in our range, we are happy to source it across India at no additional cost—simply share your product requirements and reference details with us.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, safe, and responsibly sourced products that meet UK import standards at fair and competitive prices. We work closely with certified and trusted suppliers to ensure consistency, reliability, and compliance throughout the export process.

By focusing on transparency, ethical sourcing, and dependable export practices, Slaavno aims to build long-term partnerships with UK businesses seeking reliable sourcing solutions from India.

Uncomplicated & Hassle-Free Sourcing Process

1

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your sourcing requirements. You can connect with us via a call, email, or chat to discuss product specifications, quantities, timelines, and expectations.

2

Supplier Identification

Based on your requirements, we identify suitable manufacturers or suppliers from our existing partner base or carefully onboard new verified sources as needed.

3

Detailed Sourcing Report

Within a few weeks, we provide a comprehensive report covering product pricing, documentation, quality standards, and mutually agreed Incoterms—ensuring full transparency.

4

Sample Evaluation (Optional)

Once you are satisfied with the report, product samples can be arranged for evaluation if required, before proceeding with bulk orders.

5

Secure Payment

Payments are processed securely via SWIFT transfer. Our banking partners have global operations, including offices in the UK, ensuring reliability and ease of transaction.

6

Shipment & Delivery

Shipment is arranged via air or sea freight based on the agreed Incoterms, product type, volume, and urgency, ensuring compliance and timely delivery.
