About Us

Slaavno is an India-based sourcing and buying support company focused on helping UK businesses navigate Indian suppliers with clarity and confidence.

We are not a marketplace, trading house, or seller of goods. Our role is to act as your independent sourcing partner in India—supporting you throughout the supplier discovery and buying process while ensuring transparency at every stage.

We assist UK buyers by identifying suitable manufacturers and suppliers, facilitating communication, supporting negotiations, coordinating samples, and helping manage quality and compliance expectations. All commercial agreements, payments, and shipments take place directly between the buyer and the supplier.

By working purely in the buyer’s interest, Slaavno helps reduce sourcing risks, save time, and enable better buying decisions when sourcing from India. While our primary focus is supporting UK businesses, we also assist buyers from other international markets on request.

Our Mission

Our mission is to become a trusted buying support partner for UK businesses sourcing from India.

We aim to simplify cross-border sourcing by providing transparent supplier identification, ethical sourcing practices, structured quality coordination, and dependable on-ground support in India—without taking ownership of trade or goods.

By acting strictly in the buyer’s interest, Slaavno helps UK companies build reliable, long-term supplier relationships in India while maintaining control, compliance, and clarity throughout the sourcing process.

Product Categories

Simple & Transparent India Sourcing Support

1

Requirement Discussion

We start by understanding your sourcing requirement — product specifications, target pricing, quantities, compliance expectations, and timelines. You can connect with us via call, email, or video meeting.

2

Supplier Identification

Based on your requirement, we identify suitable manufacturers or suppliers from India. We focus on supplier capability, experience, certifications, and fit for your UK market needs.

3

Sourcing & Costing Report

You receive a detailed sourcing report covering Supplier details, Indicative pricing & MOQs, Quality standards, Lead times, Commercial terms (Incoterms discussion support). This ensures full clarity before you engage directly with suppliers.

4

Sample Coordination (If Required)

If you wish to proceed, we assist with coordinating samples between you and the shortlisted suppliers, supporting communication and follow-ups until evaluation is complete.

5

Negotiation & Order Support

Once you select a supplier, we support price discussions, specifications finalisation, and commercial alignment — acting in your interest, not as a seller. "Contracts, payments, and purchase orders remain directly between you and the supplier."

6

Production & Dispatch Coordination

During production and dispatch, we help coordinate updates with suppliers and assist with communication related to quality checks, timelines, and readiness for shipment. "Logistics, shipping, and payments are handled directly by you or your appointed partners."
