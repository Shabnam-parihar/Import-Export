About Us

Slaavno is a growing Import-Export trading company based in India. We act as a link between the manufacturers, suppliers, farmers, & artisans in India and the buyers in the UK. We are establishing our sourcing network to provide a wide range of products. Currently, we are able to source multiple products in various categories; however, if you are interested in a particular product that is not in our range, we are happy to find it for you free of charge across India. Simply share your product description and pictures with us.

Mission—We aim to provide high-standard-quality, safe, and environmentally responsible products at a fair price from our certified sources and export them to the UK to our valued customers.
